# Overview

Throughout the Angel One engagement, my responsibilities extended
far beyond feature implementation.

The role evolved over multiple years and included frontend
engineering, backend integrations, WordPress development,
performance optimization, SEO engineering, enterprise search,
API integrations, production support and technical collaboration.

Rather than working on isolated tasks, I contributed to the
continuous evolution of one of India's leading fintech platforms.
