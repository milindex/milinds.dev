# Executive Summary

Angel One was one of the largest and longest-running engagements in my
career, spanning multiple years of continuous engineering work across
India's leading fintech platform.

The project involved developing and maintaining a large ecosystem of
customer-facing applications, financial content platforms, market-data
experiences and internal engineering improvements.

Unlike a traditional website project, Angel One required continuous
delivery of new features while simultaneously improving performance,
SEO, platform stability and developer experience.

Throughout the engagement, I contributed across frontend engineering,
backend integrations, WordPress development, enterprise search,
performance optimization, API integrations and production support.

# About The Product

- Equity Trading
- Mutual Funds
- IPO Investments
- Market Research
- Company Analysis
- Financial Education
- Investment Tools
- Market Data

The platform serves millions of investors through web and mobile
applications while publishing thousands of financial content pages.

# Why This Project Matters

Angel One represents one of the strongest examples of long-term product
engineering within my career.

continuous ownership of an evolving enterprise platform where
performance, scalability, search visibility and engineering quality
were equally important.

The breadth of work demonstrates the ability to contribute across
multiple engineering disciplines while supporting a high-traffic,
business-critical fintech product.
