# Overview

Angel One is a large-scale enterprise fintech platform consisting of
multiple customer-facing products, internal CMS systems, APIs,
search infrastructure and SEO-focused content platforms.

The technology stack evolved over multiple years while supporting
high traffic, performance-critical workloads and continuous feature
delivery.
