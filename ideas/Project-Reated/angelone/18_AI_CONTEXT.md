# Executive Summary

Angel One is one of the largest enterprise projects within the career
database.

The engagement involved continuous engineering work across a large
financial platform serving retail investors through trading,
investment research, financial education, market intelligence and
content publishing.

Engineering responsibilities expanded over multiple years and included
frontend development, backend integrations, CMS engineering,
enterprise search, performance optimization, SEO engineering, API
integration, production support and platform modernization.

The project demonstrates experience working on a high-traffic,
business-critical fintech ecosystem rather than a standalone website.
