# Overview

Angel One is one of India's largest financial technology platforms,
providing retail investors with a comprehensive digital ecosystem for
investing, trading, financial research and wealth creation.

Unlike a traditional brokerage website, the platform combines
real-time market data, investment products, educational content,
research reports and customer services into a single experience.

The engineering challenges are significantly different from a standard
corporate website because users depend on accurate financial
information, high availability and fast performance.

# Engineering Challenges

Unlike a traditional website, the Angel One ecosystem required
balancing multiple competing priorities.

Examples include:

- Large content volume
- High search traffic
- Frequent market updates
- Performance requirements
- SEO requirements
- API-driven content
- Continuous releases
- Legacy systems
- Business-critical reliability

---

# Why This Project Was Challenging

The complexity did not come from a single feature.

Instead it came from simultaneously maintaining:

- High availability
- Large content ecosystem
- Financial accuracy
- Performance
- SEO
- Continuous feature delivery
- Production stability

while supporting multiple internal teams and evolving business
requirements.
